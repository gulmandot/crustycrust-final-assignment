Our Story

Discover the passion and craftsmanship behind Crusty Crust Bakery

The Crusty Crust Journey

2018

Crusty Crust started in Shivani Nagar’s home kitchen, supplying freshly baked sourdough and pastries to a few local cafés and friends in the neighborhood.

2020

Word of mouth spread, and we began partnering with more cafés while setting up our first pop-up stalls at weekend markets and small events around town.

2022

With a loyal following, Crusty Crust expanded its offerings to include custom event catering and seasonal specialties, becoming a favorite at weddings and community fairs.

2024

Launched our online ordering system and expanded delivery services to reach more customers.

Today

Still proudly home-based,continuing to innovate while staying true to our artisanal roots, with a focus on quality and sustainability.

"Our mission is to create exceptional baked goods that bring joy to our community while respecting traditional methods and sustainable practices."

— Shivani Nagar, Founder

Our Philosophy

Quality Ingredients

We source the finest ingredients, including organic flour, local dairy, and seasonal produce. We believe that exceptional baked goods start with exceptional ingredients.

Traditional Methods

We honor time-tested baking techniques, including long fermentation for our breads and hand-lamination for our pastries. Some things simply can't be rushed.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental impact through composting, minimal packaging, and supporting local farmers who practice sustainable agriculture.

Community

We believe in being a positive force in our community, from donating unsold bread to local food banks to hosting workshops that share our knowledge and passion.
